1.1 Introduction
The rapid growth of digital technologies has transformed professional training, en-
couraging organizations to adopt online platforms for learning and certification. Howe-
ver, many existing solutions lack either local adaptability or full integration of essential
services. This project aims to design an intelligent platform for managing professional
training and certifications by centralizing course management, enrollment, assessments,
certifications, events, payments, and e-commerce within a unified system. Based on a
microservice architecture, the platform will ensure scalability, security, and an impro-
ved user experience while meeting the needs of learners, trainers, administrators, and
companies.
1.2 Analysis of Existing Applications
1.2.1 National Solutions
GoMyCode
oPositioning :
GoMyCode is a technology training platform focused on employability, offering
intensive programs based on project-driven learning.
oStrengths :
• Strong local presence that improves accessibility for learners.
• Mentorship model that enhances motivation and engagement.
• Practical approach enabling learners to build a professional portfolio.
oWeaknesses :
• Smaller course catalog compared to international platforms.
• Less advanced HR and compliance management (audit, advanced reporting).
• Pedagogical standardization may vary depending on cohorts and instructors.

1.3 Critique of Existing Solutions
The analysis of existing platforms highlights the rapid evolution of digital learning
environments, yet several limitations persist. International platforms such as Coursera
offer diverse courses and strong academic recognition but often lack adaptation to lo-
cal contexts and job market needs. In contrast, national platforms better address local
demands but typically provide smaller catalogs and less advanced technological infra-
structures. This study also identifies common gaps, including the absence of a unified
platform integrating training, certifications, payments, and e-commerce, limited analy-
tics for decision-making, insufficient collaborative spaces, and difficulties in balancing
personalized learning with standardized pathways. These findings justify the develop-
ment of an intelligent platform that combines the technological strength of international
solutions with the flexibility of local platforms.
1.4 Proposed Solution and Added Value
To address the limitations of existing platforms, we propose the development of an
intelligent platform for managing professional training and certifications. The system
will centralize key processes such as course management, enrollment, evaluation, certi-
fication, payments, collaboration, and e-commerce within a single digital environment.
Built on a microservices architecture, the platform will ensure scalability, flexibility, and
maintainability. Its added value lies in providing a unified ecosystem, advanced analytics
for decision-making, collaborative spaces to enhance user interaction, and personalized
learning experiences. Moreover, the platform will combine international technological
standards with local adaptability, making it both efficient and relevant to regional needs.

1.5 Functional Requirements
Functional requirements define the core features of the platform for managing e-
learning activities, certifications, events, and e-commerce services.
—Module 1 : User Management and Authentication
- User registration and account creation.
- Secure authentication with email verification.
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and permission management.
- User profile management and password reset functionality.
—Module 2 : Course Management
- Creation and modification of training programs.
- Organization of courses into structured modules and lessons.
- Creation and management of exams and quizzes.
- Advanced training categorization and tagging.
—Module 3 : Discussion Forum
- Pedagogical space for creation of publications (posts).
- Hierarchical comments on publications.
- User reactions on posts and comments.
- Content moderation (deletion and modification by authors).
—Module 4 : Messaging Management
- Private messaging system and internal conversations.
- Real-time and email-based notifications.
- Detailed message history and communication logs.
- Personalized notification preferences.
—Module 5 : Event Management
- Creation and planning of pedagogical events and webinars.
- Automated registration management and participation tracking.
- Intelligent automatic reminders for upcoming sessions.
